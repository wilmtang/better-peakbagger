Upgrade your Peakbagger.com experience.

Better Peakbagger seamlessly captures your Garmin and Strava activities, turning them into confidence-ranked ascent drafts—so you can spend less time logging data and more time in the mountains.

Features:
• One-Click Logging: Instantly convert Garmin Connect and Strava activities into review-ready Peakbagger drafts.
• 3D Terrain Maps: View peaks and routes in gorgeous, free 3D (powered by open-source Mapterhorn).
• Advanced GPX Analyzer: Get interactive elevation profiles (by distance or time), detailed route metrics, and Strava-style chart-to-map hover tracking.
• Beta Filters: Filter and sort ascent lists instantly by trip reports, GPS tracks, or external links.
• Built for You: A beautiful Dark Mode, plus shared settings for themes, units, and beta signals.

Strictly Private:
No analytics. No telemetry. No ads. No developer data servers. Ever.
